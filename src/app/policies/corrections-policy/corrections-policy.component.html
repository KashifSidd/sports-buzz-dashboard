Corrections Policy

Sportsbuzz looks to guarantee that all errors in its content are redressed rapidly and with full divulgence. Sportsbuzz moreover welcomes its readers to inform the publication group of any errors they spot in any of the articles. Any article that's found to have a typo, spelling or syntactic mistake, should be instantly modified by a part of the staff.

There's no necessity to include a rectification note in such cases; the article would instead contain an auto-generated line at the beat saying it was altered at the specific date and time.

In case a writing contains a material error with regard to any of the realities, numbers or quotes specified in that, it should be expeditiously redressed by a part of the staff taken after by the expansion of a correction note at the conclusion of the article. The redress note ought to be examined in this way: “An earlier version of the article incorrectly expressed that ___. The same has been corrected on particular time and date.”
